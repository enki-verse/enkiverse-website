About ENKIVERSE

The foundation for liberating human potential through art and creation.

Our Story

ENKIVERSE Foundation takes its name from Enki, the ancient Sumerian deity known as the god of water, wisdom, and creation. In Mesopotamian mythology, Enki was a forerunner of the Greek Prometheusâ€”a benevolent force who gifted humanity with the tools and knowledge to transcend their limitations.

This legacy inspires our mission: to provide artists, creators, and communities with the resources, freedom, and support needed to unlock their fullest potential. We believe that by nurturing artistic expression and creative innovation, we can contribute to the evolution of human consciousness and culture.

Core Values

  • Freedom - Artistic freedom without constraints, boundaries, or limitations
  • Fertility - Creating environments where creativity flourishes abundantly
  • Creativity - Celebrating innovation, imagination, and artistic excellence

Our Vision

Artist Support

Supporting emerging and established artists through grants, residencies, exhibitions, and collaborative opportunities. We're committed to removing financial and logistical barriers that prevent creators from pursuing their passion.

Innovation

Fostering innovation across artistic disciplines by connecting artists with cutting-edge technologies, sustainable practices, and interdisciplinary collaborations that push creative boundaries.

Community Building

Creating intentional communities where artists and creators can live, work, and collaborate in supportive environments that nurture long-term creative and personal growth.
